Who We Welcome

Society labels people.
We see potential.

Every person arriving at our door carries a story the world often refuses to hear. We exist to listen — and to act. ROOT was built on the principle that exclusion is the real failure, not the people being excluded.

We partner with employers who share our belief that hiring from the margins is not charity — it is smart, sustainable, and deeply human.

⚖️

Justice-Involved Individuals

Those with criminal records — misdemeanors or felonies — who have served their time and are ready to rebuild their lives with meaningful employment.

Fair Chance Hiring
🧠

Mental Health Survivors

People navigating depression, anxiety, PTSD, bipolar disorder, and other conditions who deserve workplaces designed for real human beings, not performances.

Mental Health Inclusive

People with Disabilities

Individuals with physical, cognitive, or sensory disabilities who are frequently overlooked by traditional hiring systems despite their skills and drive.

Accessibility First
🌱

Others Facing Barriers

Long-term unemployed, survivors of homelessness or addiction, those aging out of foster care — anyone society has pushed to the margins.

Barrier-Free Pathways
The cracks in concrete are not failures of the concrete. They are invitations for something living to push through.
01

Radical Dignity

We do not rescue people. We stand beside them. Every individual who walks through our process is treated as capable, intelligent, and deserving — because they are.

02

Systemic Honesty

The systems that marginalize people are broken, not the people themselves. We name that plainly and work within the world as it is while fighting to change it.

03

Rooted in Earth

Our environmental commitment is inseparable from our human one. We prioritize green employers and model carbon-conscious operations because we cannot heal people on a dying planet.

The Process

A path built for you,
not around you.

Traditional recruitment was never designed with you in mind. ROOT's process is different at every step — low-barrier entry, trauma-informed support, and employers who have signed our Fair Chance Pledge before they ever read a résumé.

01

Open Application

No background check upfront. No résumé gatekeeping. Just you, your story, and what you want to build next.

02

Human Intake Call

A real conversation with a ROOT advocate — not an algorithm. We learn what you need, not just what you've done.

03

Skills Mapping

We find what you're good at and translate that into opportunities, including roles you may have never considered.

04

Matched Placement

We introduce you only to employers who have committed to fair, respectful, sustainable hiring practices.

Our Environmental Commitment

We refuse to separate people from planet.

Marginalized communities bear the heaviest burden of environmental destruction while benefiting the least from its rewards. ROOT holds these injustices together. Every partner employer undergoes our Green Screening — a rigorous review of environmental practices before they can access our community.
